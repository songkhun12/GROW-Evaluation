## Discussion

* **Kindergarten:** JMTES-JES mean differences are positive in ELA (2.59) and negative in Math (-0.76).
* **Grade 1:** JMTES-JES mean differences are positive in ELA (3.64) and negative in Math (-0.88).
* **Grade 2:** JMTES-JES mean differences are positive in ELA (0.03), Math (0.49) and negative in no subjects.
* **Grade 3:** JMTES-JES mean differences are positive in ELA (4.96), Math (2.61), Science (7.09) and negative in no subjects.
* **Grade 4:** JMTES-JES mean differences are positive in ELA (7.89), Math (8.19), Science (7.95) and negative in no subjects.
* **Grade 5:** JMTES-JES mean differences are positive in ELA (6.52), Math (6.79), Science (8.28) and negative in no subjects.

Because observations are pooled across waves, these summaries describe overall score levels rather than causal program effects. The bivariate regressions reproduce the school mean differences as regression coefficients and should be interpreted descriptively unless supported by the stronger designs in the grade-specific analysis files.
