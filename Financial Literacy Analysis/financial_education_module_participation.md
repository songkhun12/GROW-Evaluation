# Financial Education Module Participation Among Pre-Post Financial Literacy Students

## Source and matching approach

- Pre-post financial literacy dataset: `Financial Literacy Analysis/all_grades_financial_literacy_scores.csv`.
- Student incentive tracker: `SROI/Data/GROW Student Incentive Tracking (as of 2.3.26) (PW_ 2025Data).xlsx`.
- Students were matched using the de-identified `student_id` value in the financial literacy score files and the tracker `Student ID` field. The financial literacy score files also include `at_risk_state_report_id`, which is reported below as the state/report identifier.
- In the tracker workbook available in this repository, the financial education module field is labeled **"Incentive 2- 1 HR Fin. Literacy Module Online"** and appears in **column J** on the grade-level sheets. Column F is Race/Ethnicity and column O is the attendance incentive field ("Miss three days or less"). To preserve the audit trail for the requested columns, columns F and O are included in the detailed table below, while the module completion flag is based on the column labeled as the financial literacy module.
- A check mark (`✔`) in the financial literacy module column was coded as **Yes**; blanks or `X` were coded as **No**.

## Summary

- Total students in the pre-post financial literacy dataset: **57**.
- Matched as having completed the financial education module: **33**.
- Not marked as completing the financial education module: **24**.
