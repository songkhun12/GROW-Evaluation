# Economics Arkansas Financial Activities Participation Among Pre-Post Financial Literacy Students

## Source and matching approach

- Pre-post financial literacy dataset: `Financial Literacy Analysis/all_grades_financial_literacy_scores.csv`.
- Student incentive tracker: `SROI/Data/GROW Student Incentive Tracking (as of 2.3.26) (PW_ 2025Data).xlsx`.
- Students were matched using the de-identified `student_id` value in the financial literacy score files and the tracker `Student ID` field. The financial literacy score files also include `at_risk_state_report_id`, which is reported below as the state/report identifier.
- Economics Arkansas financial activities were coded from the two user-requested tracker columns: **column E** and **column N**. A check mark (`✔`) in either column was coded as **Yes**; blanks or `X` in both columns were coded as **No**.
- Technical note: in the extracted workbook XML, the first requested activity check field appears at column I and the user-identified column N check field appears at column O; the table below labels them as requested columns E and N to match the workbook view used for review.
